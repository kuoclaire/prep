Agent UX Case Study

Prep Meeting Assistant

Prep addresses meeting preparation anxiety: workers struggle to walk into meetings confidently because critical context is buried across siloed tools they don't have time to search before their next calendar event starts.

Project Goals

  • Make prep effortless & fast: Users should feel fully prepared in under 2 minutes—without opening a single additional tool.
  • Surface missed context: Proactively flag changes, new attendees, and recent decisions before they become live embarrassments.
  • Intentional sharing: Context sharing remains an explicit choice—never happening automatically or without user awareness.
  • Stay invisible until needed: Stay out of the way and surface only when genuinely useful, earning trust by respecting attention.
